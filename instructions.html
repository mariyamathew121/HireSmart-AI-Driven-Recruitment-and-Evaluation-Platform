Welcome to the Recruiter Dashboard

Follow these steps to manage job postings and candidates efficiently:

How to Post a Job

  • Click on "Add Job Description" from the sidebar.
  • Fill in the job title, description, qualifications, and other details.
  • Click the "Submit Job" button to publish the job listing.

Viewing Candidates

  • Click on "View Candidates" from the sidebar.
  • Browse through the list of applicants who have applied for the job.
  • Shortlist candidates based on their resumes and qualifications.

Accessing Uploaded Resumes

  • Click on "Uploaded Resumes" from the sidebar.
  • Download and review the resumes submitted by applicants.
  • Use the information to make informed hiring decisions.

Managing Job Openings

  • Click on "Open Job Openings" from the sidebar.
  • View the list of active job postings.
  • Edit or remove job postings as necessary.

If you need further assistance, please contact support.
